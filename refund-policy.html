Refund Policy

1. Confirming an Adventure

No refund objections will be accepted after 7 days following the adventure.

2. Club Subscription Cancellation

  • Subscriptions are non-refundable unless the host does not add any adventures for the entire month.
  • Users can cancel at any time.

3. Adventure Ticket Cancellation & Refund

Important Conditions

  • Registration deadline
  • Minimum required number of attendees
  • All conditions met but the host does not deliver

Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Ticket cancellation: Allowed anytime
  • Refunds are issued only if:
    • The ticket is canceled before the registration deadline.
    • The minimum required number of attendees is not met.
    • The host does not deliver despite all other conditions being met.

Refund Decision Process

  • If no refund objections are raised within 7 days after the adventure, funds are sent to the host.
  • If at least 2/3 of attendees request a refund within 7 days, all attendees will be refunded.
  • If fewer than 2/3 request a refund within 7 days, payment is delayed while the host provides objective proof (pictures, chats, etc.). If proof is insufficient, all tickets will be refunded.
  • If no refund requests are made within 7 days, the host is paid as planned.

4. Required Elements for Refund Processing

  • The registration deadline is non-modifiable after the adventure is created.
  • The registration deadline must be set at least one day before the adventure.

5. How to Request a Refund

  • Click the refund button for automatic refunds before the registration deadline.
  • If the registration deadline has passed, use the refund button and contact support with proof within 7 days.

6. Actions Taken by the Platform

  • Enable easy reporting of adventure-related issues.
  • Require club hosts to provide a Kakao ID or phone number when creating a club.
