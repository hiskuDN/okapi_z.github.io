Okapi-Z Child Safety Policy

Effective Date: April 2025

Last Updated: April 2025

At Okapi-Z, we are deeply committed to providing a safe and trusted space for all users, especially minors. While our platform is not primarily designed for children, we recognize that some users may fall within vulnerable age groups, and we take proactive measures to prevent any form of child exploitation, abuse, or harm.

1. Zero Tolerance for Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation (CSAE)

We have a zero-tolerance policy for any behavior, content, or activity that involves child sexual abuse material (CSAM) or facilitates CSAE. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Sharing or uploading explicit or suggestive images/videos of minors
  • Grooming, solicitation, or any communication with minors intended for sexual exploitation
  • Organizing or promoting events involving child exploitation

Any violation of this policy will result in:

  • Immediate account suspension or ban
  • Reporting to relevant local and international law enforcement authorities

2. In-App Reporting Mechanism

Users can report suspicious, inappropriate, or abusive behavior directly through the Okapi-Z app via:

  • The "Report" button on any user profile, adventure listing, or chat
  • Our dedicated support email: glodymatayi5@gmail.com

All reports are taken seriously, prioritized, and reviewed by our trained moderation team, who are equipped to escalate issues to the appropriate authorities.

3. Monitoring and Moderation

We maintain a manual moderation process supported by user reports and host verification procedures. We are currently working toward integrating automated moderation tools such as keyword filters and behavioral monitoring.

4. Compliance with Laws and Cooperation with Authorities

We fully comply with:

  • Korean child protection laws
  • Global child safety regulations, including COPPA, GDPR (as applicable), and guidelines from INTERPOL and local cybercrime units

Okapi-Z works closely with law enforcement agencies when necessary to support investigations and share relevant evidence, while protecting user privacy under the law.

5. Age Restrictions and User Consent

  • Users must be at least 18 years old to create an Okapi-Z account or host adventures.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18.
  • If we learn that a user under 18 has registered without guardian consent, their account will be deactivated, and any personal data will be erased promptly.

6. Data Privacy and Protection for Minors

  • We follow strict data minimization principles, avoiding unnecessary collection of personal data.
  • We do not allow any user to publish a minor's personal or contact information.
  • Our backend systems are protected by industry-standard encryption and access controls to prevent unauthorized access or data leakage.

7. Staff Training and Safety Awareness

All Okapi-Z staff, moderators, and volunteers undergo:

  • Regular training on child safety protocols
  • Workshops on recognizing and handling CSAE
  • Updates on new threats, grooming patterns, and digital safety trends

We also provide guidance to adventure hosts on how to maintain a safe physical environment during offline meetups.

8. Community Guidelines & Enforcement

Our community guidelines emphasize:

  • Respect, consent, and inclusivity
  • Strict consequences for harassment, predatory behavior, or nudity involving minors

Repeated or severe violations may result in:

  • Permanent bans
  • IP blocking
  • Reporting to cybercrime and child safety organizations
